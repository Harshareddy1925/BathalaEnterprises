About Bathala Enterprises

Your reliable partner in property management since 2019.

Our Mission and Vision

Bathala Enterprises was founded in 2019 by BATHALAPALLI SATHISH KUMAR REDDY with a clear mission: to provide a comprehensive, one-stop solution for all property-related needs in Bengaluru. We are dedicated to delivering top-notch services with a commitment to customer satisfaction, transparency, and expertise.

Our vision is to be the most trusted property management company in the region, known for our reliability and the quality of our work. Whether it's ensuring the safety of your premises, maintaining your property, or helping you find the perfect home with zero brokerage, we strive for excellence in everything we do.

What Makes Us Special?

  • Customer-Centric Approach: Your satisfaction is our highest priority.
  • Expert Team: We have a dedicated team of skilled professionals for every service.
  • Zero Brokerage: We believe in fair deals, connecting you directly to properties without hidden fees.
  • Comprehensive Solutions: From security to sales, we cover all aspects of property management.
